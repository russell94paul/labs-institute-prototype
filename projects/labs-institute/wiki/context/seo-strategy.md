## Technical SEO

- Static site for fast load (53% mobile users abandon after 3s)
- Responsive meta viewport
- Compress images, lazy-load embeds
- JSON-LD structured data: MusicGroup, Event
- Google Business profile (Manchester)
- Consistent URL structure: `/artists/[slug]`
