# Value Proposition & Commission Model

## Industry Standard

Standard artist management commission is ~10% of gross booking fees. Some agencies charge 15-20%.

## Labs Positioning

Frame as outcome-based, not cost-based:
- "We only succeed if you succeed — standard 10% commission, with real-time career tracking"
- Emphasise reinvestment: commission goes back into artist development, marketing, better events
- Transparency: DJs see exactly where money goes (ties to brand value)

## "With Labs vs Without Labs" Comparison

Show on About page:

| Metric | Without Labs | With Labs |
|--------|-------------|-----------|
| Gigs per year | 5-10 (self-booked) | 20-40 (managed pipeline) |
| Social growth | Organic only | Data-driven strategy |
| Contracts | Informal/verbal | Professional, transparent |
| Pricing | Guesswork | Market-rate benchmarked |
| Career path | Unstructured | Milestone-driven roadmap |
| Press/PR | None | Targeted outreach |
