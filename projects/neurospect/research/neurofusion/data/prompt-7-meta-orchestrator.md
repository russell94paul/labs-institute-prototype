## Transfer, update cadence, and recursive improvement

On transfer learning, the available evidence is encouraging. QuantNet explicitly aims to learn market-agnostic trends that improve market-specific trading strategies, while more recent work on zero-shot financial time-series forecasting uses meta-learning to learn initialisations that adapt rapidly to new tasks with minimal data. There is no openly documented system that fully matches your definition of a **self-improving heterogeneous ensemble for quant trading**. The closest in-domain public precedents are partial: adaptive model-selection and ensembling methods for financial time series, transfer across systematic trading strategies, mixture-policy portfolio selection with meta-learning, and evolutionary alpha/model search in portfolio pipelines.
