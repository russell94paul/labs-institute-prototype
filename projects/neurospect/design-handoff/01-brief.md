# NeuroSpect Marketing Site — Design Brief

## Product

NeuroSpect is the first AI-native platform built exclusively for ICT (Inner Circle Trader) and Smart Money Concepts traders. It replaces 5-7 disconnected tools with a single integrated intelligence layer: AI coaching, trade journaling, quantitative backtesting, and automated trading — all powered by an LLM that actually understands ICT methodology.

## Target Audience

1. **Active ICT students** ($29/mo) — Learning ICT methodology, want AI coaching and journal
2. **Serious traders** ($99/mo) — Trading live, want backtesting and risk limits
3. **Strategy researchers** ($199/mo) — Want full EdgeLab, walk-forward optimization, NSLM experiments
4. **Quant-curious traders** ($349/mo) — Building hybrid ICT + quant models
5. **Educators/teams** ($499/mo) — ICT mentors who want to turn their content into an AI coach

## Design Vision

**"Bloomberg terminal meets trading dojo."** The site should feel like the future of trading — not a generic SaaS landing page. Think: dark, data-rich, alive with subtle motion. Every visualization should make traders think "I need this in my workflow."

### Key Impressions to Create

- **Authority** — This is a serious platform built by people who understand ICT deeply
- **Intelligence** — The AI is real, grounded, and ICT-native (not a ChatGPT wrapper)
- **Data-driven** — Everything is backed by numbers, backtests, and statistical rigor
- **Premium** — Worth the subscription. High production value. Flagship-quality.
- **Trust** — Source citations, disclaimers, safety architecture. Not hype.

### What It Should NOT Feel Like

- Generic SaaS template with stock photos
- Crypto/meme-coin marketing (no moonshots, no lambos)
- Overly complex or cluttered — progressive disclosure, let the data breathe
- A "coming soon" page — it should feel like the platform already exists

## Site Purpose

1. **Communicate the product vision** — What NeuroSpect is and why it matters
2. **Capture waitlist signups** — Email collection for early access
3. **Demonstrate capability** — Interactive visualizations that prove the platform's value
4. **Differentiate** — Show why NeuroSpect isn't just another trading tool

## Deployment

- Static site (no backend auth)
- Cloudflare Pages or similar
- Waitlist form captures emails (backend TBD — Cloudflare Workers KV, Resend, or Mailchimp)

## Current State

The site is built in Astro + Tailwind CSS + Chart.js with 6 pages and ~30 components. It's functional but needs professional design polish, enhanced visualizations, and interaction refinements to reach flagship quality.
