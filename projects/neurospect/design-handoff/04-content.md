### AI Coach Demo (chat mockup)

**User message:** "I entered short on ES at a FVG but price swept my stop. What did I miss?"

**AI response:**
> Looking at your entry, there are two issues from the **Silver Bullet Entry Model**:
>
> 1. The FVG you targeted was **unmitigated but inside a prior breaker** — this creates a conflict zone where price is likely to sweep.
> 2. Your stop was placed at the FVG high, but the model specifies stops above the **last swing high before displacement**.
>
> Sources: Silver Bullet Entry Model · FVG Classification Rules · Your Trade #247

**Entry Model Checklist:**
- [PASS] Time window (10:00-11:00 ET)
- [PASS] Displacement present
- [PASS] FVG identified
- [FAIL] No conflicting PD array
- [FAIL] Stop above swing high

**4 value props alongside:**
1. Source-Grounded Citations — Every answer cites the specific ICT concept, entry model, or course module it came from. No hallucinations.
2. Entry Model Checklists — Deterministic validation against all 7 machine-readable entry models. Pass/fail on every criterion.
3. Personalized to Your Trading — The coach reads your journal. It knows your recurring mistakes, your best setups, and your risk profile.
4. Available 24/7 — Pre-session prep, mid-session questions, post-session review. Your mentor sleeps. NeuroSpect doesn't.
