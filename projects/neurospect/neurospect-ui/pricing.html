Frequently Asked Questions

When will NeuroSpect be available?
We are currently in private beta, building phase by phase. Prop Shield (Phase 3) is the first paid feature — targeting release later in 2026. Join the waitlist to get early access and be notified when your tier is ready.
What is NeuroFund Elite?
NeuroFund Elite is a company-sponsored reward and recognition program for top-ranked traders on NeuroScore. It is funded from NeuroSpect's revenue. It is NOT a prop firm, NOT pooled capital, and NOT an investment vehicle. Eligible traders may receive recognition rewards and access to advanced research tooling.
Can I use NeuroSpect with any prop firm?
Prop Shield is designed to work with the major CME futures prop firms. We support Tradovate integration (TopstepTrader, Apex, etc.) and can track custom prop firm rules. More integrations are added each phase.
Is the AI coach trained on ICT concepts?
Yes. NeuroSpect Mentor uses a RAG (Retrieval-Augmented Generation) system that pulls from our ICT knowledge wiki, your personal playbooks, and your trading history. All coaching citations link back to the original source material.
Does NeuroSpect provide trade signals?
No. NeuroSpect is an analytical, educational, and research tool. The ICT Event Engine detects and classifies events — it does not tell you to buy or sell. All trading decisions are entirely your own. See our full risk disclaimer below.
Can I cancel anytime?
Yes. No contracts, no lock-ins. Cancel anytime from your account settings. Annual plans are refunded pro-rata for unused months.

Past performance of any strategy, backtest, or simulation is hypothetical and not indicative of future results. Backtesting has inherent limitations and does not account for slippage, liquidity, or real-world execution constraints. NeuroFund Elite is a reward program, not an investment vehicle.
