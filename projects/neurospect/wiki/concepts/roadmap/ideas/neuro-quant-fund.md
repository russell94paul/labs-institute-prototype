# Neuro-Quant / Neuro-Fund

> **Naming note:** "Neuro-Quant" and "Neuro-Fund" are placeholders. Better names may be picked later.

Two related long-term concepts:

- **Neuro-Quant** — automated strategy research: backtesting, forward testing, feature engineering, walk-forward validation. Could eventually support systematic strategy development on top of the entry-models library.
- **Neuro-Fund** — helping traders divert profits from prop-firm accounts into long-term investment vehicles for financial security/freedom.

## Why it matters

Neuro-Quant is the natural extension of having a structured strategy library — the entry models in [[concepts/entry-models/README]] are already machine-readable. Neuro-Fund is the strongest-possible value proposition: turn prop trading from a treadmill into wealth-building.

## Compliance / risk notes

- **Neuro-Quant** alone is research-grade and low-risk *as long as it's not packaged as investment advice or signals.*
- **Neuro-Fund is investment-vehicle territory.** Pooled capital, RIA registration, prospectuses, jurisdictional restrictions. Cannot be promised to users without licensed structure (broker-dealer, RIA, fund administrator).
- Marketing implications: do not let "Neuro-Fund" appear in user-facing copy until the regulatory shape is decided.

## Dependencies

- Mature trade data and strategy library.
- **Legal counsel + regulatory pathway** before any commitment.

## Open questions

- Neuro-Quant: in-app feature vs. separate research tool?
- Neuro-Fund: partner with an existing RIA vs. build internal capability?
- Jurisdictional scope (US-only vs. global).
