# In-App Course / Education Section

Structured learning surface inside the app: psychology, execution, journaling, risk management, strategy review. Sourced from the existing course KB ([[concepts/course/README]]).

## Why it matters

The course material already exists in the wiki. Surfacing it in-app gives Neurospect a self-contained education arc, makes onboarding richer, and creates a natural home for future educational content without bloating the journaling or coaching surfaces.

## Dependencies

- [[concepts/roadmap/ideas/ui-design-exploration]] for a non-bloating layout.
- Content pipeline from wiki → app (similar problem to AI Coach prompt sync — needs a deliberate sync path).

## Open questions

- Course progression tracking per user?
- Integration with the AI Coach (coach references lessons by ID).
- Free vs. gated tiers when monetisation arrives.
