# Vertical AI Platform for Retail / Prop Traders

Strategic positioning: Neurospect is **the** vertical AI product for retail and prop-firm traders, not a generic journaling SaaS with AI sprinkled on. Every roadmap decision is filtered through this framing.

## Why it matters

Vertical AI products win on domain depth. The ICT-specific course KB, structured entry models, and ICT-fluent coach are not features — they're the moat. This page exists to remind future sessions that adding generic features (e.g., support for non-ICT styles, generic tag systems, broad TA tools) erodes the position.

## Dependencies

- Evidence from [[concepts/roadmap/ideas/platform-consolidation]] that the integration thesis holds.
- A user base that self-identifies as ICT/SMC + prop-firm.

## Open questions

- Where's the line between "vertical depth" and "narrow market" — are non-ICT futures traders an adjacent expansion or a distraction?
- Pricing model implications (vertical AI products typically command higher per-user pricing).
