Vol II Class 1: Power of Three (Accumulation – Manipulation – Distribution) 
 
1. Core Concept 
 
The "Power of Three" is not about a ﬁxed, picture-perfect pattern (like those seen 
on social media), but about market narrative. 
 
A daily candle represents three phases: 
o Accumulation → Smart money builds positions (below the daily open). 
o Manipulation → Market deceives retail traders into selling (price driven 
below the open, making them think it’s bearish). 
o Distribution → Smart money unloads long positions as retail chases 
breakout buys (above daily open). 
 
2. Candlestick Lesson 
 
A candlestick is not just a color — it is formed by Open, High, Low, Close 
(OHLC). 
 
Whether it’s bullish or bearish depends on the relation between open and close 
(not the chart color). 
 
This daily candle framework is the foundation for reading the Power of Three.

3. Market Behavior 
 
Retail psychology: 
o Below daily open = retail sees weakness, tries to short. 
o Above daily open = retail sees strength, tries to buy. 
 
Smart money uses this psychology: 
o Let’s retail sell under open → smart money accumulates longs. 
o Pushes market higher above open → distributes longs to retail breakout 
buyers. 
 
Essentially, manipulation = trapping retail, accumulation = smart money 
positioning, distribution = proﬁt-taking. 
 
4. Intraday Application 
 
The concept repeats multiple times a day with session opens: 
o 00:00 (Midnight open) 
o 08:30 
o 09:30 
o 13:30 
 
Rule: 
o If bullish → look to buy when price is below opening prices. 
o If bearish → look to sell when price is above opening prices. 
 
5. Key Takeaways 
 
The "Power of Three" is about narrative, not patterns. 
 
Accumulation & manipulation happen together — smart money needs retail to 
sell so they can buy. 
 
Always align trades with daily/session opens. 
 
Be patient for conﬁrmations (liquidity sweep + structure shift). 
 
Missing a trade is ﬁne — more opportunities will come. 
 
Goal isn’t “perfect entries,” but right direction + narrative.
