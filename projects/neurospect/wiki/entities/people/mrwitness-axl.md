# MrWitness-AXL (Mentor)

The Neurospect mentorship content is delivered by two instructors who co-teach every class: **MrWitness** (the lead conceptual teacher) and **Axel / AXL** (the execution specialist). Their combined approach covers theory → real-time trade review.

## MrWitness

- Primary instructor for all conceptual content
- Teaches ICT/Smart Money Concepts methodology with emphasis on **narrative** and **algorithmic price delivery**
- Teaching style: whiteboard diagrams + live chart walk-throughs, followed by homework assignments
- Believes in understanding the "why" behind every concept before applying it
- Runs live daily commentary streams (separate from the recorded class content)
- Platform: Discord (Dojo channel, Homework channel, Narrative channel)
- Known for: stressing the difference between narrative-based trading vs. pattern-based trading

## Axel (AXL)

- Execution specialist; focuses on intraday scalping at extremely low timeframes (15s, 5s, 1s)
- Teaching style: live trade reviews with step-by-step breakdown of his three-step framework
- Step-by-step framework:
  1. HTF confluence (15M or above)
  2. LTF confirmation (15M or below)
  3. Entry timeframe (1M or below) — change of structure + confirmations
- Known for: ultra-tight stop losses (10–12 ticks), high R:R (1:10 to 1:30+ regular)
- Instruments traded: primarily NQ (Nasdaq 100) and ES (S&P 500) futures
- Uses **completed zones (CZ)** — a personal concept (advanced, covered in later volumes) describing zones that don't need to be retested
