# Product Onboarding and Guided Decision Simulator

Modes:
1. New Product Build
2. Existing Product Improvement
3. Data Platform / Service Modernization

Every AI recommendation should include: recommended option, alternatives, explanation, pros/cons, implementation cost, monthly operating cost, support cost, timeline impact, scalability impact, compliance impact, risk, business/user value, dependencies, confidence score, and missing evidence.

Dynamic controls should include budget, timeline, users, scalability, compliance, automation, support level, infrastructure cost ceiling, risk tolerance, speed vs quality, refactor vs rebuild preference, and data access level.
