# LABS Institute Research Folder

Place the deep research output here.

Recommended files:

- `deep-research.md` — raw research output with citations and source notes
- `source-notes.md` — relevant links, source credibility notes, and caveats
- `assumptions.md` — distilled assumptions approved for product planning
- `competitor-matrix.md` — competitor/platform comparison

Important: the orchestrator should not treat raw research as build instructions. Convert research into assumptions, decisions, MVP scope, and acceptance criteria first.
