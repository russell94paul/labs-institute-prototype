Submit Feedback

Try the AI Chat
In the Designs tab (Layout A), scroll to the bottom and type a question in the chat. Try asking about pricing, venues, or growth strategy — the AI responds using real artist data.

Drag the Career Scrubber
In the Designs tab (Layout A), find the timeline slider and drag it from 2012 to 2026. Watch the stats, milestones, and growth percentage animate in real-time.
