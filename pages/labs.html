Lab-Intelligence + Lab-Wiki — Self-Improvement Engine
Events/Gigs → Outcomes logged → Lab-Intelligence ingests ↓ Social metrics → Periodic snapshots → Pattern detection ↓ AI generates suggestions (ranked by: ROI, complexity, growth) ↓ Chat surfaces to founder/DJ ↓ Feedback loop (accept/reject/modify) ↓ Lab-Intelligence improves ↓ Lab-Wiki captures proven patterns
Lab-Intelligence — Vector DB ingesting gig outcomes, social metrics, campaign results. Detects patterns, generates growth roadmaps, surfaces improvement suggestions ranked by complexity, productivity, ROI, and growth potential.
Lab-Wiki — Structured knowledge base: what works at which venues, optimal pricing by location, best promo strategies. Searchable by founder and DJs.
Chat — Founder ↔ DJ messaging with AI assistant pulling context from both systems.
