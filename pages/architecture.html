How Value Compounds Over Time
Month 1-3
Foundation
• DJ profiles populated with baseline KPIs
• First gigs logged with outcomes
• Venue profiles started in Lab-Wiki
• Social metrics begin daily snapshots
• AI suggestions are generic (limited data)
AI accuracy: ~40%
Month 3-6
Pattern Recognition
• Lab-Intelligence spots trends (best content, venues, times)
• Pricing suggestions calibrated from real outcomes
• Lab-Wiki has 20+ venue profiles with notes
• Workflow library has 50+ saved runs
• AI suggestions becoming useful (~60% accepted)
AI accuracy: ~65%
Month 6-12
Compounding Intelligence
• Cross-artist insights: what worked for DJ A helps DJ B
• Pricing engine auto-adjusts from 100+ data points
• Lab-Wiki is the definitive guide to the industry
• Content strategy personalised per DJ
• AI suggestions are trusted (~80% accepted)
AI accuracy: ~80%

Better suggestions lead to better outcomes.
Better outcomes generate better data.

The more you use Labs, the smarter it gets. The smarter it gets, the more valuable it becomes.
